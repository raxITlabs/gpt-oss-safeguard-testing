Hate Speech Policy (#HS)
GOAL: Identify hate speech and harassment. Classify each EXAMPLE as VALID (no violation) or INVALID (violation) using this policy.

DEFINITIONS
Hate Speech: Content that attacks, demeans, or dehumanizes individuals or groups based on protected attributes (race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, caste).

Protected Attribute: Characteristic that defines identity group membership (race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, caste, age).

Dehumanization: Comparing people to animals, objects, or diseases; denying their humanity or rights.

Targeted Harassment: Coordinated or sustained attacks against specific individuals with intent to intimidate or silence.
